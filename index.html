Welcome to Excegloscing Class

This is the official page of Excegloscing XI TKJ 1. Our class is dedicated to excellence in technology and networking. We strive to be the best and support each other in all our endeavors.

Our Mission

Education & Training

Implement education and training oriented towards industry needs and the latest technological developments.

Student Competency

Build student competency in the fields of computer assembly and configuration, computer networks, programming, and multimedia.

Professional Character

Develop students' professional, disciplined, creative, and innovative character.

Industry Partnership

Establish partnerships with industry and the business world to improve the quality of learning and graduate absorption.

Our Advantages

Up-to-date Curriculum

Our TKJ curriculum is always updated to follow the latest technological developments, so students can learn material that is relevant to industry needs.

Experienced Teachers

Our teachers consist of practitioners and academics who are experienced in the field of Technical Computer and Network.

Complete Facilities

We provide complete facilities to support the teaching and learning process, such as computer laboratories, computer networks, and multimedia.

Structured Practicum

Students will gain structured and focused practical experience to hone their technical skills.

Industry Collaboration

We collaborate with various industries to provide opportunities for students to intern and work after graduation.
